Our Story

From a garage workshop to thousands of happy pets, here's how we got here.

How We Started

From a Hackathon to Your Pet's Collar

It all started in 2019 when our founder, Alex Chen, was tinkering with 3D printing at a weekend hackathon. His golden retriever, Cooper, needed a new collar after destroying yet another one at the dog park.

Three prototypes later, Cooper had the most comfortable, durable collar he'd ever worn — and Alex had a business idea. What if we could use precision manufacturing to create pet accessories that were both better for pets and better for the planet?

Our Mission

Better Products, Better Planet

We believe pets deserve accessories that are as thoughtfully designed as they are beautiful. Every PetCraft product starts with a question: how can we make this better for your pet and better for the world they'll grow up in?

  • Made from plant-based, biodegradable materials
  • Zero-waste manufacturing process
  • 1% of every sale goes to animal shelters
  • Designed to last, not to be replaced
