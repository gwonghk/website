Products & Materials

All PetCraft products are made from PLA (polylactic acid), a biodegradable material derived from renewable corn starch. PLA is non-toxic, hypoallergenic, and safe for pets. It's also incredibly durable and lightweight, making it perfect for everyday use.
Our products are designed to withstand the rigors of daily pet life. We use high-quality, food-safe PLA that's been tested for durability. Our collars feature reinforced attachment points and our tags are built to last. Most customers find their PetCraft products outlast traditional alternatives by a significant margin.

Yes! Our products are made from non-toxic, pet-safe materials. However, we recommend supervising young puppies or kittens with any accessory until they're old enough not to chew on it. For puppies under 6 months, we recommend our lightweight starter collars that are specifically designed for their smaller size and developing neck.
Most PetCraft products can be cleaned with mild soap and warm water. For stubborn dirt, you can use a soft brush. Avoid harsh chemicals or placing items in the dishwasher, as extreme temperatures can affect the material over time. For tags with engravings, gently wipe around the text to maintain legibility.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, contact us and we'll arrange a return or exchange. For personalized items, we can offer an exchange for a different size or color, but custom designs are final sale unless there's a manufacturing defect.
We're sorry your order didn't arrive in perfect condition! Please email us at support@petcraft.example with photos of the damaged item and your order number. We'll ship a replacement at no cost to you within 1-2 business days.
Once we receive your returned item, refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Please allow an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or card issuer.

Sizing & Fit

Use a flexible measuring tape (or a piece of string you can measure later). Measure around your pet's neck where the collar will sit, keeping two fingers between the tape and your pet's neck. This ensures a comfortable fit. If between sizes, we recommend sizing up.

Size Guide:
• XS: 8-12" neck (small cats, puppies)
• S: 12-16" neck (small dogs, large cats)
• M: 14-18" neck (medium dogs)
• L: 18-24" neck (large dogs)
• XL: 24-30" neck (extra large breeds)
Absolutely! Size exchanges are free within 30 days of purchase. Just contact us at support@petcraft.example with your order number and the size you'd like. We'll send you a prepaid return label and ship the new size as soon as we receive your original item.
